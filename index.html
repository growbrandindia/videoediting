Frequently Asked Questions

Do you edit both long-form and short-form content? +
Yes! I specialize in both. Whether it's a 1-hour podcast sync or a high-energy 30-second TikTok, I have dedicated strategies for both formats.
How do I send you my raw footage? +
We typically use shared Google Drive or Dropbox folders. Once you sign up, I'll send you an onboarding link to securely upload your files.
What is your turnaround time? +
For short-form content (Reels/Shorts), turnaround is usually 24-48 hours. Long-form videos and complex motion graphics take between 3-5 business days.
Do you use AI in your editing process? +
Yes, I leverage cutting-edge AI tools for audio cleanup, automated captioning, and generating unique visual concepts to speed up workflow and enhance quality.
